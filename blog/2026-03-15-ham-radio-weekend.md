The rain was loud enough to hear through the window but soft enough to make the house quiet.

I spent most of Saturday rotating through a few antennas and making tiny adjustments.

Nothing dramatic happened, but that is part of the point: repeatable setups, cleaner contacts, and one more notebook page of observations.
